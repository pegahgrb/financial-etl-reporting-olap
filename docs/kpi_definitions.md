# KPI Definitions

## Revenue

Sum of all transactions mapped to the `Revenue` reporting group.

## Cost of Goods Sold

Sum of all transactions mapped to the `COGS` reporting group.

## Operating Expenses

Sum of all transactions mapped to the `OPEX` reporting group.

## Gross Profit

`Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold`

## Operating Profit

`Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold - Operating Expenses`

## Department Expense

Department-level sum of expense transactions.
