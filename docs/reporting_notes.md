# Reporting Notes

This project is designed like a small finance reporting mart that could support:

- monthly management reporting
- finance dashboards
- OLAP-style drill-down by department or account group

## Practical BI use

A BI team could use these outputs to power:

- monthly P&L reporting
- department cost reviews
- trend analysis
- executive dashboarding

## Portfolio framing

This is a public-safe reconstruction of the kind of finance ETL and reporting work involved in building structured management reporting pipelines.
